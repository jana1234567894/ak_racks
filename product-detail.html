Product Description

We are engaged in offering a wide range of Free Standing Supermarket Display Racks to our clients. Our range of products is widely appreciated by our clients for their durability, strength, and cost-effectiveness. These racks are manufactured using high-grade medium duty steel with precision engineering.

The supermarket display rack features 6 adjustable shelves that can hold up to 150-200kg per shelf, making it perfect for displaying packaged goods, groceries, and retail products. The perforated backboard allows for flexible product arrangement and hook placements.

Our racks are powder-coated with anti-corrosive coating to ensure long service life even in high-humidity retail environments. Available in standard grey and orange finish, with custom color options available for bulk orders. Installation support and comprehensive warranty included.
